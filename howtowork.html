Frequently asked questions

Our painting team is trained and mandated to do professional packaging, masking and daily cleaning throughout the painting job. They will move back the furniture after the service, and do final post job cleaning to ensure there are zero paint marks or stains once the job is complete.

Any such requirements will be taken care of by the assigned painting team. You do not need to worry about sourcing materials.

Our supervisor will supervise your painting service daily. You can always contact him directly for any issues, questions, or assistance needed.

The payment is divided into two phases: You'll pay 35% upfront (refundable), and the remaining 65% after we've completed the service to your satisfaction. You can inspect for any peels or cracks in the paint, ensure a smooth wall finish, and confirm that your space is ready to live in before making the final payment.
